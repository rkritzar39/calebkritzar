About Us

A small business created with a big purpose.

Welcome to Caleb’s Merch Store — a small business created with a big purpose. From the beginning, our goal has been simple: to support, celebrate, and uplift people with disabilities through meaningful merchandise that inspires connection and positive change.

Caleb’s Merch Store was built on the belief that every person deserves to be seen, valued, and included. We aim to spark conversations, raise awareness, and bring representation where it’s often overlooked.
