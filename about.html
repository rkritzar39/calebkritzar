My Story

I’m Caleb Kritzar — a developer and entrepreneur from Ohio. My life has involved a lot of change and adjustment, which taught me adaptability and persistence. I found my place in tech through hands-on learning, problem solving, and building projects that feel modern and useful.

I’m especially interested in front-end development and practical features that make websites easier to use. I also care deeply about inclusion, which is a big part of why I created Caleb’s Merch Store — a mission-driven business that supports and celebrates people with disabilities.
